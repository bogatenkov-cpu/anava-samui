Landmark Development - First Freehold Oceanview Condominium

ANAVA Samui is a landmark project set to become the first and only freehold oceanview condominium in Koh Samui in over two decades. This exclusive development combines sophisticated architectural design with innovative living concepts, featuring a unique wrap-around swimming pool that seamlessly connects the entire property, creating a stunning water-themed living experience unlike any other on the island.

Uniquely positioned on pristine Bangrak Beach with direct beachfront access, ANAVA Samui integrates residential living, vacation opportunities, and investment potential. The project is developed by Zhongfa Property (HK) Limited in partnership with a world-renowned hotel management company, ensuring top-tier services and unmatched hospitality standards throughout the development.

As the only condominium in Koh Samui offering investors a guaranteed fixed return on investment, ANAVA Samui presents an exceptional opportunity for those seeking both a luxurious tropical home and secure financial returns. With freehold ownership options for foreign buyers and strategic proximity to Samui International Airport, this beachfront property offers unparalleled investment potential in Thailand's most sought-after destination.
